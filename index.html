Our Services

HVAC Services

HVAC system operates on three main principles - Heating, Air Conditioning & Ventilation, i.e., it conditions the air in the area occupied by the facility and ventilates the air by diluting and separating harmful contaminants present inside the facility.

Industrial Ventilation

CAE provides assistance in ventilation services which helps in the exchange and circulation of air within the premises to dispense high indoor air quality by eliminating unpleasant smells and excessive moisture to the outside atmosphere keeping the indoor air free of stagnation.

Centralized Air Conditioning

Inside homes, offices, or other big indoor areas like Healthcare Facilities, Supermarkets, Hotels and so on. These units are designed to offer more comfort for improving air level/quality. We offer the best Centralized Air Conditioning solutions for all properties.

Chiller Plants

Installation of the Chiller plant is required for reciprocating heat from the water to the closed refrigerant system. Later the refrigerant will be injected into the location where unwanted heat can be moved to the atmosphere. Excellence & Multiple facilities are key factors due to which there is a higher demand for chiller plant installation services.

Packaged Air Conditioning

Choosing the right air conditioner and placing the air conditioners in the right direction are helping factors to offer the best results. Solutions for Air conditioning encompass factors like Heating, Cooling, Humidity, Ventilation, and Humidity control.

VRV and VRF Air Conditioning

VRV / VRF is a modern and sophisticated technology that helps in reducing electricity consumption by regulating the refrigerant flow of liquid. Hence, the machine performs efficiently according to human occupancy, orientation, the direction of the sun, and heat-generating equipment with a payback period of approximately three and half years.

UVGI For HVAC

Ultraviolet Germicidal Irradiation (UVGI) is an effective method of air and surface disinfection when integrated with the HVAC system of the building. It utilizes ultraviolet (UV) light to deactivate the Virus by disrupting and destroying its DNA so they are unable to perform its vital cellular functions and ultimately gets killed.
